Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the GenZ experince.

How does mobile ordering work?

Simply download our app, select your store, and customize your drink. You can track your order in real-time and skip the line when you arrive.

Do you offer catering for events?

Yes! We provide "Coffee Boxes" and pastry platters for office meetings, parties, and photo shoots. Message us for our current catering menu.

Can I earn rewards for gift cards?

Absolutely. Linking your gift card to your GenZ Coffee account ensures you earn points on every purchase, even when paying with credit

Are there vegan milk options?

We stock Oat, Almond, and Soy milk at no extra charge. We believe the perfect cup shouldn't have a surcharge for your lifestyle choices.
