Small-Batch, Big impact.

Our story began with a simple observation: the coffee industry felt too clinical, too rushed. We wanted to bring back the warmth. Our beans are ethically sourced from woman-owned farms in Ethiopia and roasted in small batches right here in the city to ensure every bag is peak freshness.

Precision Roasting

We treat roasting like an art form. Every origin gets a custom profile
to highlight its unique flavor notes, from jasmine tea to toasted caramel.

100% Ethical

We pay 25% above fair trade prices because quality starts with the
well-being fof the people who grow it. No compromises, ever.

Eco-Conscious

Our bags are fully compostable and our
cafes run on 100% renewable energy.

Community

1% of all sales goes back to local youth art
programs in our neighborhood.
